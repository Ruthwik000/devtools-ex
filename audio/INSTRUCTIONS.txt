HOW TO ADD ALERT SOUND
======================

1. Find or create an alert sound (1-3 seconds, MP3 or WAV format)

2. Name it exactly: alert.mp3 or alert.wav

3. Place it in this audio/ folder

4. Run: npm run build

5. The sound will play when Focus Detection detects a phone

RECOMMENDED SOUNDS:
- Short beep or notification sound
- Not too loud or jarring
- 1-3 seconds duration

FREE SOUND SOURCES:
- https://freesound.org/ (search "alert" or "notification")
- https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/
- https://www.zapsplat.com/

TESTING:
1. Enable Focus Detection in the extension
2. Show your phone to the webcam
3. The alert sound should play when detected
4. Sound has a 5-second cooldown to prevent spam
